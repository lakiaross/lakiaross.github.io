Trust Logo

A research group at GTRI needed a logo for a project involving trust between devices. The device communication could be mobile to mobile or sensors to sensors, focusing on the information devices share with each other and how does one device trust what the other is telling it. The logo conveys this communication process; the squares representing units, devices, sensors, etc. The arch is the trusted exchange between them. The word “trust” is literally bridging the gap between the squares.

  • Client: Georgia Tech Research Institute
  • Date: Sept. 2014 - Jan. 2015
  • Service: Logo Design
